## Central Icon System (mapping only)

The icon ontology (`design/icons.yaml` · `public/brand/icons.json`) carries a
`central:` field naming the corresponding glyph in the
[Central Icon System](https://iconists.co/central) (Iconists) for
operator-licensed PRIVATE/atelier surfaces. **No Central artwork is included
in, served by, or redistributed from this repository** — their license
forbids public redistribution; only the name mapping (plain text) appears
here.
