## What hurt

- The error code registry design went through three iterations before settling
  on `NikaCode` as the dual enum (category + numeric). The first two attempts
  leaked the Display impl into downstream crates, violating the L0 boundary.

## What we learned

- In Rust, error enums that are `#[non_exhaustive]` from day one save
  downstream crates from breaking-change cascades. Add it before anyone
  depends on you, not after.
